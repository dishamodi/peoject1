Cristiano Ronaldo scored a hat trick as Portugal defeated Switzerland 3-1 to reach the final of the inaugural UEFA's Nations League. Ronaldo

French Open 2019: In golden era of tennis rivalries, Nadal-Djokovic stands out,
says Mats Wilander

Even though Roger Federer is still going on strong, former world no. 1 Mats Wilander thinks that the most intense rivalry is between top seed,
Novak Djokovic and 11-time French Open winner, Rafael Nadal. Roger Federer’s gripping four-set victory over friend and compatriot Stan Wawrinka
has set up a tantalising semi-final showdown with clay-court king Rafael Nadal, the 39th meeting between two of the most successful players of all time.
